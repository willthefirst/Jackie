Once upon a time there lived a girl named Jackie, who lived in a big house...

...well, not that big.

In fact, Jackie and her mother were very poor. They had little money, little food, and a little house. All they did have was a cow, who provided little milk.

One day, Jackie's mother sent her daughter to sell their the cow. But Jackie, on her way to the market, met a strange man, who held in his hand three magic beans. He told Jackie that the beans were more valuable than any amount of money, because if planted, they would transform into a giant beanstalk. Jackie was mystified, and decided to trade her cow for the beans. But when she returned home with no money, no cow, and just three little beans, her mother was furious. So furious that she grabbed the little beans out of Jackie's little hand, threw them out the window, and sent poor Jackie to bed, without the little dinner that there was.

Jackie wished she had just sold the cow. She fell asleep with guilt, ashamed that she had not followed her mother's orders.

But the next morning...

...and higher still.

A giant, green beanstalk towered outside her little house. Curious Jackie, without her mother hearing, jumped out of the window to her room, and started to climb the beanstalk.

She climbed higher...

...and higher...

And when she thought she could climb no more, Jackie reached the top, where she discovered a long road leading to a little castle in the distance.

But as Jackie approached the castle, she realised that it was not little at all: it was giant. It had a giant door with a giant doorknob, and was surrounded by giant trees. Jackie, so accustomed to little things, felt littler than ever. Curious Jackie ventured into the house, and saw a sack of gold coins, a golden harp that played itself, and a goose that laid golden eggs. Thinking that these giant objects could give her family all the money they would ever need, Jackie seized each one. But suddenly, at the very moment that she closed her pouch after taing them, she heard a giant roar from a giant voice.
